The royal game of Ur

  1. The aim of the game to get all your pieces around the board
  2. Each player has 7 pieces
  3. Each players roll one at a time, they may move their piece on the board based on the number rolled. Their own pieces may not overlap.
  4. The player's pieces are safe on the top and bottom region of the board. On the central region, if an piece lands on an enemy piece, that enemy piece is sent back to the begining and off the board.
  5. Tiles marked with a star allows you to play twice in a row and the central star tile keeps your piece safe in case the enemy piece lands on it.
